Block elements appear on the screen as if they have a line break before and after them.
For example the p, h1, h2, h3, h4, h5, h6, ul, ol, dl, pre, hr,blockquote, and address
elements are all block level elements. They all start on their own new line, and
anything that follows them appears on its own new line.
